**Effective Date:** Oct 28th, 2025

## 1. Purpose

This statement clarifies Asaman Technologies Ltd.’s position on Artificial Intelligence (AI) and safeguarding within our platform. While Asaman does not currently deploy AI or machine learning algorithms, explaining our commitments before introducing any such features is important.

## 2. Current Practices

### a. No AI Processing

Asaman does not use artificial intelligence, machine learning, or automated decision-making to process personal data, student information, or institutional content.

### b. No Automated Profiling

Asaman does not analyze, score, or predict student behavior, performance, or preferences through automated tools.

### c. Human Oversight Only

All activity management, scheduling, reporting, and communication functions are performed by humans using rule-based logic, not AI.

## 3. Future-Ready Commitment

Asaman is developing a governance framework for the responsible integration of AI in the future. Our principles will include:

- **Transparency:** Schools will be clearly informed of any AI-enabled features before they are activated.
- **Human Control:** All AI suggestions or results will require review and approval by school
- **Data Protection by Design:** No personal or student data will ever be used to train AI models.

## 4. Safeguarding Standards

Regardless of AI usage, Asaman follows strict global safeguarding practices:

- Zero data collection directly from children under 13
- Mandatory parental or institutional consent for all student records
- Encrypted storage of all personal data
- Staff training in data ethics and child protection

These safeguards comply with COPPA, GDPR Article 8, China’s PIPL, and Singapore’s PDPA.

## 5. Review And Update

This Statement will be updated before any AI functionality is introduced. A revised AI & Safeguarding Usage Notice will be published on **www.asaman.net/terms-and-policies** and communicated to all partner schools.

Asaman Technologies Ltd. does not currently utilize AI in its products. Our platform is 100% human-driven, compliant, and transparent. As we innovate, we will introduce AI only with full institutional oversight, clear consent, and built-in safeguards.
